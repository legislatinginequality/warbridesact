80 S. 1264

May 9, 1947

Sen. William Chapman Revercomb (R-WV)

A BILL To extend the time within which alien fiancées or fiancés of members of the armed forces may be admitted into the United States.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, That the first section of the Act entitled "An Act to facilitate the admission into the United States of the alien fiancées or fiancés of members of the armed forces of the United States", approved June 29, 1946, is amended by striking out "July 1, 1947" and inserting in lieu thereof "December 31, 1947".
