80 S. 1962

January 9, 1948

Sen. John Chandler Gurney (R-SD)

A BILL To extend further the period during which the admission into the United States of alien fiancées or fiancés of members of the armed forces of the United States may be facilitated.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, That the authority conferred upon the Secretary of State and the Attorney General under the provisions of the Act entitled "An Act to facilitate the admission into the United States of the alien fiancées or fiancés of members of the armed forces of the United States", approved June 29, 1946 (60 Stat. 339), is hereby revived and shall continue in effect to June 30, 1948, midnight.
